Meet Our Partner

Deloso Veterinary Clinic is a trusted provider of pet grooming, veterinary check-ups, and animal care. With their commitment to excellent service and animal well-being, they partnered with us to create Project PetPal — a smart appointment and inventory management system tailored to their growing needs.

Project PetPal streamlines appointments, tracks inventory, and enhances overall customer experience — giving Deloso Vet more time to care for pets.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is PetPal Scheduler?

PetPal Scheduler is an online platform that allows you to book pet grooming, veterinary, and other pet care services easily and conveniently.

2. What services do you offer?

We offer Grooming Services, Vaccination, and more.

3. How much is a basic grooming service?

Basic Bath & Brush starts at ₱350. Full Grooming Package starts at ₱600.

4. How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by logging in to your account and selecting your desired service and schedule.

5. Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through your account dashboard.

6. What are your vaccination prices?

Deworming: ₱200, 4-in-1 Vaccine: ₱450, 5-in-1 Vaccine: ₱500, 8-in-1 Vaccine: ₱700, Anti-Rabies: ₱350.

7. Do you accept walk-in customers?

Yes, but we recommend booking online to secure your slot and avoid waiting.

8. How can I contact PetPal for support?

You can use the "Message Us" button on the website or email us at delosovet@gmail.com.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, GCash, Credit Card, and other major payment methods.

10. Where is Deloso Veterinary Clinic located?

GAP Building Villa Magdalena 2, Camarin Road, North Caloocan, Philippines.
