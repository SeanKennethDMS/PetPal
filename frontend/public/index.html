Meet Our Partner

Deloso Veterinary Clinic is a trusted provider of pet grooming, veterinary check-ups, and animal care. With their commitment to excellent service and animal well-being, they partnered with us to create Project PetPal — a smart appointment and inventory management system tailored to their growing needs.

Project PetPal streamlines appointments, tracks inventory, and enhances overall customer experience — giving Deloso Vet more time to care for pets.
